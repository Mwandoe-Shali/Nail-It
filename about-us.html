The Story Behind.

Inspiration comes in many different forms. Mine came about as I was in a walk trying to find what can I do that will be compatible with my daily work.

I am a Quantity Surveyor ( A fancy name for a person who calculates what is needed for a building to be built to completion ). On construction sites the major hurdle is stock taking of what remains and what lags behind, because a slight delay may cause the trickle effect into the whole plan, thus having a good number of materials on site ensures the continuity of events.

Thus, the idea of a construction stock taking was born.
